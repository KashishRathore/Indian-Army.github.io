Honours And Awards

The Armed Forces of India are awarded many military decoration, honours and awards. The awards and honours are awarded for extraordinary bravery and courage,as well as for distinguished service during times of war and peace. For the purpose of classification, Indian Armed Forces honours and awards can be divided into Two categories:

  • Gallantry Awards
    •       i.Gallantry Awards(War Time)
    •       ii.Gallantry Awards(Peace Time)
  • Non-Gallantry Awards

Gallantry Award

The purpose of the Indian Army's Gallantry Awards is to recognise the bravery and sacrifice of military troops and officers, as well as that of other legally established Forces and citizens. This article provides a wealth of information on the prizes given to army personnel who display courage. These Indian army Gallantry awards are announced twice in a year - first on the occasion of the Republic Day and then on the occasion of the Independence Day.
