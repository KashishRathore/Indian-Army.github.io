About Indian Army

The Indian Army is the largest standing army in the world.Army Day is celebrated on 15th January every year in India, in recoginition of Lieutenant General Kodandera M.Cariappa's (later who became Field Marshal) taking over as the first Commander-in-Cheif of Indian Army.from General Francis Roy Bucher in 1949. In 2023, India will celebrate the 75th anniversary of Indian Army Day.

The primary mission of the Indian Army is to ensure national security and national unity, to defend the nation from external aggression and internal threats, and to maintain peace and security within its borders.

Formation and Structure of Indian Army

The Second Largest standing army in the world and the largest component of Indian Armed Forces. The Indian Army is Undoubtedly the pride of India. The Indian Army has 1.2 million active troops and 1 million reserve troops right now.

The Headquarter of Indian Army is in New Delhi and and is under the overall command of COAS , Chief of Army Staff. Currently COAS of India is General Manoj Mukund Narvane.
